NVIDIA shader disk cache__GL_SHADER_DISK_CACHE (+ path, 10 GiB, no auto-purge) written to /etc/environment for all games. Persists compiled shaders across runs. DXVK's own cache vars are obsolete on modern Proton, so they're not set.
